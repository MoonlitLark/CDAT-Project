Narwhals Against The World

Jeremiah A, Amilliyon W, Valentina D, and Benie N

Adaptations of the Narwhal

Narwhal's "Horn"

The Horn on a Narwhal is a Canine Tooth

        -Used to detect the attributes of sorrounding Water
        -Helps them determine whether or not the water is safe for them

Body Heat Management

        Narwhals have evolved to store blubber in order to insulate their bodies
        Lack a Dorsal fin in order to decrease their surface area and prevent heat loss

Chemial Stressors in the Environment

Harmful concentrations of Mercury have accumulated
within the tundra as a result of improper disposal of
Gas and Oil waste. pushing the Narwhals out of their
natural habitat.

Solutions
